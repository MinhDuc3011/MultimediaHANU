SEO Marketing

1. All about SEO Marketing

- Search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of improving your website to increase visibility on popular search engines such as Google and Bing. In this certification course, you'll learn all things SEO, including website optimization, link building, keyword research, and much more.

- You’ll learn the latest techniques from real-world experts using hands-on exercises that fit into your schedule. Our range of courses will appeal to both beginners and advanced learners who want the most current information.

- In a nutshell, SEO is the practice of increasing traffic on a website through organic search results. But not just any traffic — qualified traffic is needed. After all, getting millions of visitors doesn’t help if they are not interested in what your site has to offer.
