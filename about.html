About Us

Bachelor's Degree Program in Multimedia

1. Introduction to Multimedia Faculty

Multimedia communication is a discipline that applies Information Technology (IT) in the creation, design and development of multimedia and interactive products and services for the fields of communication (advertising, broadcasting, etc.) video...), business (marketing, e-commerce...), education (online training, virtual reality...), entertainment (video games, movies, music...). Multimedia industry has the intersection of knowledge from many fields such as Information Technology, Fine Arts, Communication, Marketing ... but the most important requirement for learners of this major is to have passion and connection. creative customs. If you are the type of person who is passionate about challenges and does not like boring stereotypes, studying multimedia will be an option worth considering.

2. Advanced training program

- Selective references from international training programs of the US and Australia, meeting modern trends and new human resource needs in the domestic and foreign media industry.
- Provide background knowledge of three industries: Information Technology, Communication, Marketing; In-depth knowledge of communication, advertising, marketing, e-business, digital content design, multimedia product development programming...
- The content of practice is diverse, the number of practice hours accounts for over 60% of the entire course content. A variety of electives are geared towards training students' career skills. There is a commitment to participate in training activities and ensure output after graduation.

3. Fluent in English

- Train students to have good English skills (reaching English proficiency level 4 according to the 6-level Foreign Language Competency Framework for Vietnam), being able to study and work in a fully communicative environment in English.
- Learn foreign languages with a university with a tradition of teaching foreign languages for nearly 60 years, professional study with enthusiastic, dynamic, professional young lecturers, trained in the UK, Australia and the United States.

4. Great career opportunities

Graduates of the Multimedia Communication program meet the new requirements of the labor market for dynamic, creative human resources with foreign language ability and interdisciplinary knowledge. Therefore, there will be many opportunities for employment, learning, and self-development. After graduating, students can work in the following positions: Technical staff, managers, executives in the fields of advertising, communication and event organization; Specialist in programming, application development, design, editing and deployment of multimedia products and services; Participating in projects as a project manager for information and communication technology. Research staff, lecturers on advertising and communication at Institutes, Research Centers and training institutions.

5. Lecturers, experts and businesses teach together

- 100% of lecturers are trained abroad, actively participate in the research network of Information and Communication Technology in Vietnam.
- Specialized teaching: 12 full-time lecturers with Doctorate and Master degrees, and experts in Information Technology, Multimedia Communication, Marketing who are entrepreneurs or lecturers at training & research institutions prestige of Vietnam, UK, Australia, USA.
- Teaching foreign languages: Prestigious and experienced lecturers of Hanoi University.
